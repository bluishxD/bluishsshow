Episode 1 – Catguy

In this Episode, CatGuy talks about his journey in Web3 and NFTs, starting from getting into crypto in 2017. He shares the ups and downs of the market and how research, persistence, and passion helped him grow. CatGuy explains how his nickname came about and how he spots opportunities others might miss. He also talks about his daily habits, work ethic, and balancing personal life while building content for NFT communities. Listeners get a real look at his mindset, lessons, and advice for anyone interested in Web3. Listen Now on Spotify

About Catguy

CatGuy is a well-known NFT collector, DeGods community member, and Web3 creator focused on the Solana ecosystem. With a background in venture capital, he has built influence through collecting NFTs and since not so long ago TheNFTcat. In this episode, he shares lessons from his journey, mindset tips, and advice for aspiring creators.
