About Chris Taylor

Chris Taylor is a DJ, creative, and Web3 marketer working with Magic Eden and Phase. Starting his journey in music, he built an international DJ career before transitioning into Web3 during COVID. Today, he combines content creation, marketing, and crypto experience while continuing his creative work on the side. In this episode, Chris shares his journey from DJing to Web3, insights on building communities, and his philosophy on creating income, balance, and long-term value.
